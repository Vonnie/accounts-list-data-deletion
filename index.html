Accounts List – Data Deletion Instructions

Accounts List does not require user accounts and does not store user data on developer servers. All account information is stored locally on the user's device.

How to delete your data

  1. Open the Accounts List app.
  2. Delete any accounts you no longer wish to keep.
  3. If you created a Dropbox backup, delete the file located at accounts/accounts.json in your Dropbox.
  4. You may also uninstall the app or clear the app's storage from your device settings to remove all data.

Contact

If you have questions about data deletion, you may contact the developer through the Google Play listing.
